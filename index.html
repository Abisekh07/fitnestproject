WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

At Stretch Affect we look at the whole person ! Not just parts of your body that may have issues. Optimal or dysfunctinal movement patterns can make or break you. If a life free of limitations and injury sounds good to you, welcome to the program.

A Modern Approach
to Movement Health

We are not a physical therapy, massage, stretching, or
chiropractor office. We are not a personal training facility.

We are a premium movement health.

The quality of movement—the way you move can have
tremendous impact on alleviating pain, injury prevention,
performance, and recovery. Achieving exceptional
movement health encompasses everything Stretch Affect
has to offer: a full-body assessment, hands-on manual
therapy, corrective exercises, and tailored fitness
programming, allowing you to get out of pain and reach
your maximum potential.

We go beyond the status
quo to deliver
exceptional results

We start with the belief that everything is connected. Often times
your primary issue can stem from problems elsewhere. We don’t
just accept an issue at face value. To identify the root of your issue,
we’ve created an extensive full-body assessment that examines
how you move and identifies any deficiencies that will guide your
personalized treatment plan. Along with a physical assessment, we
feel it’s important to get to know you—we spend time one-on-one
getting to know your injury history, lifestyle, activities, habits, and
movement goals.

Each and every time you meet with Stretch Affect, you get one-on
-one time with your movement health specialist, no assistants, no self
-directed training; spend time engaging, learning, and improving
with your specialist. This individual attention not only propels you to
get better faster, but also you begin to understand how your body
should move, giving you the power to stay healthy and pain-free.

Reasons We Stand Apart :
Getting out of pain is paramount. Then we must lay a proper
foundation before progressing.
We look beyond the current problem, taking in the totality of your
body’s needs and goals to get you where you want to be.
When you change patterns, you become more efficient, prevent
injury, and maintain a healthy body. When you understand how
your body should move, you can be your own advocate for life-long change.
Functional sessions with exercises that prepare your body to
perform what it is you love to do.
Not restricted to one way of getting results. Choose from a vast
array of skills and techniques that work best for you.
No matter your stage in life, there is always time to take care of
your body and be able to do the things you love for years to come.
Boost your recovery and ensure a more consistent, pain-free performance.
